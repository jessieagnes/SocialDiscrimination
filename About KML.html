    DESCRIPTION

    Social discrimination is the unfair or discriminatory treatment of individuals or groups based on race, gender, age, religion, or sexual orientation. It often involves the exclusion or marginalization of certain individuals, denying them equal opportunities and rights. Discrimination can take many forms, including hate speech, or physical violence. It is a widespread issue that affects individuals in various aspects of life, including work, education, and health care. Addressing discrimination in society requires not only legal protections but also efforts to promote understanding, empathy, and inclusion. By fostering a culture of respect and acceptance, society can take action towards eliminating discrimination and creating a more equal and harmonious environment for all.

    AIM

    This social campaign's primary objective is to educate others about social discrimination, which is a well-known problem that revolves around social media, and to provide helpful information that demonstrates how we will be able to make better changes so that we can have a better society where people are free to express themselves. As a result, we can demonstrate to the online community that improving the world will also improve us as individuals. We intend to make improvements and encourage people to assist one another by posting positive messages on social media that demonstrate how we are able to deal with social discrimination. In order for us to have the bravery to be free and at ease in our surroundings, we want everyone to be completely confident in who they are without fear of criticism from others.

    GOAL

    We are committed to creating awareness of social discrimination, not only on race, gender, and sexuality but also on socioeconomic status. Moreover, we encourage people and communities to understand how discrimination against less privileged members affects their freedom and opportunities. We also plan to create awareness on how to address and stand up against discrimination by providing support such as sharing our gathered info’s about the topic and remind them on how physically and emotionally can give an negative affect to someone because of this issue.

    BENEFITS

    The benefits of this campaign that, they can raise awareness about different forms of discrimination, helping people recognize and understand its impact. it will provide freedom for people who experience discrimination and educate people about topics which are new to them and encourage them to participate in bringing change and also make people happier and more productive.
