    Welcome to our social campaign entitled KAMALAYAN sa MAPANGMATANG LUPUNAN or KML, a social campaign that raises a social awarenes about how individual normalize a prejudice or judgemental society.

    Our advocacy is to raise awareness to different individual that are not fully know what is social discrimination mean. Also, to help individual that experiencing and suffering from unfair treatment to their social class, color, age, sex, religion, and race. We believe that there is no individual that can judge you of being who you are that's why, join us as we speak, move, and fight to change our society from being a prejudice to judgement-friendly society. because in our society where in, You are always the main character in your own story.
